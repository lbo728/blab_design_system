# BRAND.md

## Brand Intent
BLab should feel like a calm, polished, and trustworthy design system for modern digital products.
It should help product surfaces feel intentional rather than improvised.

## Visual Personality
- Calm
- Polished
- Trustworthy
- Softly futuristic
- Intentional
- Minimal, but not sterile

## Product Tone
BLab should communicate clarity before excitement.
Interfaces should feel stable, readable, and refined.
Visual style should support confidence and ease of use.

## Density and Layout Feel
- Favor breathable spacing over cramped density.
- Prefer clear grouping and rhythm over maximum information packing.
- Use depth and hierarchy carefully to guide attention.

## Surface Philosophy
BLab may use glass-like or layered surfaces, but only when they improve hierarchy and atmosphere.
Surface treatments should support clarity, not exist for decoration alone.

## Light and Dark Mode Attitude
Both light and dark modes should preserve the same emotional tone.
Dark mode should feel calm and premium, not neon-heavy or overly dramatic.

## What BLab should feel like
- Deliberate
- Smooth
- Lightweight
- Friendly but professional
- Modern with restraint

## What BLab should never feel like
- Noisy
- Gimmicky
- Over-animated
- Randomly colorful
- Visually dense without hierarchy
